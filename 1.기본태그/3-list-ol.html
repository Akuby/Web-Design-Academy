How to Bake Some Nice Desserts.

CUPCAKES
  1. Mix flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix eggs, milk, and oil.
  3. Stir both mixtures together.
  4. Fill muffin tray 3/4 full.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes.
COOKIES
  1. Prepare some flour, sugar, eggs and salts
  2. Cream butter and sugar in large mixing bowl using Electric hand mixer.
  3. Add all other ingredients except flour, and combine well.
  4. After resting time, dust the wooden board with some flour and take the flour on board. Using Rolling pin, roll out dough about 1/4 inch thickness. Shape it using cookie cutters.
  5. Let them cool before Icing.
PIES
  1. First combine all ingredients except the water.
  2. Roll out the dough about 1/4" thick and to a few inches larger than your pie pan.
  3. Allow for an hour in the fridge or 15 min in the freezer.
  4. Bake for 20min at 400 deg (205C) with a piece of foil wrapping the crust.
  5. Recipe calls for 2c. Flour, 1tsp Salt, 2/3c. Cold Unsalted Butter (or lard), 5 tbsp. ice water
